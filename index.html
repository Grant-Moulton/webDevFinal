About us

The U-turn is a new installment to the High School this year. We have decided to make a School Store! Here you will be able to purchase Food and Merch. You will also be albe to find infromation on Sports and Clubs. This will become a Hub of Information for students and should be very valuable to each and every one of you. We ask that you maintain a good attitude towards those running it and hope you have a great experience. We will do all we can for you and do our best to keep you happy. Let's share the School Spirit and have fun. Remember above all we value our GRIT. Go Utes!
